✅ What's Fixed

  • H1 starts at top: 200px (center of hero)
  • Animates to top: 10px (inside navbar)

How It Works Now

The h1 uses position: fixed instead of sticky, which allows it to move vertically with the scroll animation. The @keyframes animates both the top property (position) and font-size simultaneously.
